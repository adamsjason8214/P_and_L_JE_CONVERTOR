Food Safety Reference

1-Hour Pizza Slice Rule

Hold Time: 60 minutes maximum

Warning: Alert at 50 minutes (10 min remaining)

Temperature: Must maintain 135°F or above during hold time

Action: Discard any pizza slices that exceed the 1-hour hold time

Critical Cooking Temperatures

  • 165°F: Poultry, Reheated Leftovers
  • 155°F: Ground Beef/Pork, Raw Eggs (hot holding)
  • 145°F: Fish, Shellfish, Whole Beef/Pork/Lamb, Eggs (immediate service)
  • 135°F: Vegetables, Pre-cooked Foods, Hot Holding

Storage Temperatures

  • 41°F or below: Cold Holding
  • 0°F or below: Freezer Storage

Two-Stage Cooling Process

Step 1: Cool from 135°F to 70°F within 2 hours

Step 2: Cool from 70°F to 41°F within 4 hours

Total Time: Maximum 6 hours total cooling time

Date Marking: Ready-to-eat TCS foods must be date marked and used or discarded within 7 days at 41°F or below

Handwashing Requirements

Duration: Minimum 20 seconds

Wash Before:

  • Your shift begins
  • Handling food
  • Putting on clean gloves

Wash After:

  • Using the toilet
  • Handling raw foods
  • Taking a break/smoking
  • Coughing, sneezing, eating, drinking
  • Cleaning/taking out trash

Based on Delaware General Health District Food Safety Standards
